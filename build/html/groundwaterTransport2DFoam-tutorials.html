Validation Case: Coupled Water and Solute Transport in a 2D Watershed

Starting from the steady-state simulation, this case models the coupled transport of water and solute. A passive tracer is injected with a source term \(Q_{tracer} = 5 \times 10^{-6} \text{ kg}. \text{m}^{-2}. \text{s}^{-1}\) for 40 years, while the infiltration rate varies over time, influencing both water and solute distribution.
